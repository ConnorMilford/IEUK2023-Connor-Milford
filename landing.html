Why are net-zero emissions important for agricultural SMEs?


The food industry is one of the most wasteful in the world.

Produces > 900 million tonnes of waste per year.

Produces > 1/3 of greenhouse gas emissions.

Beef farming uses nearly 80% of the worlds agricultural land

1kg of beef requires 15,414 litres of water to produce.

If it continues like this, emissions will rise by between 30-40%.
